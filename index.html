My name is Truman. I am a prospective software engineer with interest in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and C# (C-sharp). I have made interest calculators, utilized API's for social media platforms, and created script based log books for backend.

If you follow the link for "Websites", it will redirect you to my Github portfolio and detail various team projects I have worked on, and some others that are currently in progress. I used to row for college and wanted to give to the team in some way. So I reached out to the current leadership and proposed the idea of donating a website to them for prospective members to reference the team with. Some things included on the website utilize weathers API's of reoccuring regetta's they participated in, a schedule of the teams work out. And the sitting president is able to login and make changes to the schedule as they please.

Some other interest of mine include photography, camping, disk golfing, canning, visiting museums, and trying different restaurants. And recently, coding; I have been practicing since June 2022 and haven't stopped since. There has been a lot of development since including the many projects listed under the websites button.
